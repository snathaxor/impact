Who Are We?

Impact RCM LLC. was founded in 2015 as Handmade Logix LLC. but our team has a collective experience of over 5 decades. Some of them have been around since 2002. Over the course of 21st century, we have worked on a large number of small to big sized clients from a large number of specialization.

Impact RCM was started with the vision to change the face of the RCM industry by providing the excellence in client services. Since its foundation, Impact RCM has been providing its clients with the highest level of satisfaction in their revenue stream. The solutions provided are focused on innovation, quality with advanced technology and on reducing the cost for the clients.

At Impact RCM, our processes include automation, monitoring and analytics. We believe that medical billing is going through an era of technological advancement where previous manual efforts will be overtaken by technology, AI and automation thus a direct impact on your revenues. We aim to achieve efficiency thus our medical billing processes are based on technoloigically advanced processes.

We take great pride in our outstanding customer service, striving to deliver top-notch services to our clients consistently. Through meticulous effort, our team ensures the provision of accurate, timely, and efficient billing services tailored to the unique requirements of each client.
