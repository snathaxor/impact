Technology Based Medical Billing

At Impact RCM, our processes include automation, monitoring and analytics. We believe that medical billing is going through an era of technological advancement where previous manual efforts will be overtaken by technology, AI and automation thus a direct impact on your revenues. We aim to achieve efficiency thus our medical billing processes are based on technoloigically advanced processes.

Credentialing

Our credentialing team setup the providers with government or commercial payers depending on the need. With accurate paperwork, we pave the way for seamless operations which eventually helps in revenue increase.

Patient Registration

Patient registration is most critical of all steps in RCM. Verifying eligibility & benefits at this stage maximize the chances of reimbursement. Our billing protocols ensure the verification of patient eligibility and benefits before claims go out to the payers.

Medical Coding

Recent and frequent changes in ICDs and CPTs pose a significant challenge for all healthcare professionals in terms of medical coding. We have a team of CPC coders who ensure that your billing stays compliant and at the same time accurate to generate more revenue.

Charge Capture

Efficient charge capture ensures accurate recording of services provided while minimizing billing errors thus reducing AR. Automated processes and meticulous tracking in charge capture enhances financial transparency and reimbursement.

Aggressive Follow-Up

After ensuring the efficiency in claim submission, we ensure that payments have been received and posted in the system. Again, we ensure automation and ERA setups are created where possible.

Reporting & Softwares

Reporting and transparency is our top priority. Our team is well versed with most of the billing softwares in industry specially their reporting and analysis thus ensuring timely updates.
